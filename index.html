FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a detail take?

It depends on the service. A basic wash and wax takes 1-2 hours. A full detail (interior and exterior) typically takes 3-5 hours. Ceramic coating can take a full day. We'll give you a time estimate when you book.

Do I need to be present during the detail?

Not required, but you're welcome to watch! Many customers enjoy seeing the transformation. You can go about your day—just leave us access to your vehicle and we'll handle everything. We'll call or text when we're finished.

What vehicles do you detail?

Cars, trucks, SUVs—domestic and import. From daily drivers to luxury vehicles, work trucks to show cars. If you're not sure whether we can detail your specific vehicle, just call and ask.

How much does it cost?

Depends on the service. We give you a clear quote before starting any work—no surprises. Basic washes start affordable, full details are priced based on vehicle size and condition. Call us with what you need for an estimate.

What's your service area?

We're based in Denver, Colorado and serve the surrounding area. This includes nearby towns and suburbs in the region. If you're not sure if we can reach you, give us a call—we'll let you know if you're in range.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Cash and most common payment methods. Payment is typically due when the detail is complete. For larger jobs like ceramic coating, we may ask for a deposit. Just ask when you call and we'll work it out.
